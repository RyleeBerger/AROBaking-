Coconut Scones

  • 1 egg
  • 1/4 cup of coconut oil
  • 1/4 cup of unrefined suger
  • 1 1/2 cups of almond flour
  • 2 tsp of arrowroot powder/flour, or cornstarch
  • 1 tsp of vanilla
  • 1/4 of a cup of unsweetend shredded coconut
  • 1 tsp of aluminum free baking powder
  • 1 tsp of coarse suger to sprinkle the scones, optional
  • Blueberries or chocolate chips.

    Instructions:

  1. Preheat the oven to 350ÂºF.
  2. Line a 9 inch round baking pan with unbleached parchment paper all the way up to the sides of the baking pan or take a baking tray and line it with parchment paper.
  3. In a mixing bowl, add the egg, sugar and coconut oil and mix until combined
  4. Add the remainder of the ingredients except the blueberries and mix until combined. Then add either the blueberrys or the chocolate chips.
  5. Press the batter into the prepared baking pan and sprinkle some coarse sugar over the top. Bake for 35-40 minutes.
  6. Another option is to scoop the batter with a ice cream scooper and place on parchment paper and bake for 30-40 minuets
  7. Let the scones cool for at least 10-15 minuets and then eat and enjoy!
