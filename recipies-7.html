Pumpkin Cinnamon Sugar Donut Holes

  • Ingredients:

  • 1/2 cup (66 grams) coconut flour
  • 3/4 cup (75 grams) almond flour
  • 1 1/4 teaspoons baking soda
  • 3 1/2 teaspoons pumpkin pie spice
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 4 large eggs, room temperature
  • 7 tablespoons (98 grams) coconut oil or unsalted butter, melted
  • 1/2 cup (120 milliliters) maple syrup
  • 2 tablespoons coconut sugar or brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup (120 grams) canned pumpkin puree (not pumpkin pie filling)
  • 2 teaspoons vanilla extract
  • For the cinnamon sugar:
  • 1/3 cup (67 grams) coconut sugar or granulated sugar
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground cinnamon

      • Instructions:

    1. Preheat the oven to 350 Â°F.
    2. Line a mini muffin pan with 24 muffin liners.
    3. In a large bowl, mix together the dry ingredients (coconut flour through salt).
    4. In a separate medium bowl, mix together the wet ingredients (eggs through vanilla).
    5. Add the dry mixture to the wet mixture and stir just until combined.
    6. Pour the batter evenly into the muffin liners, filling each liner until almost completely full. You'll likely have enough batter for another six donut holes.
    7. Bake for 11-13 minutes or until a toothpick inserted in the middle comes out clean. Turn out onto a wire rack to cool completely. These need to rest for at least an hour before serving, both because of taste and texture.
    8. When ready to serve, mix the cinnamon with the sugar in a small bowl. Remove the liners and roll the donut holes in the cinnamon sugar. Warning: Do not leave sugar on over night.
    9. Eat and enjoy!
