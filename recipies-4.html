Peaunut Butter Blossom Cookies

    Ingredients:

  • 1 cup of peanut butter (creamy recommended)
  • 1 cup of suger
  • 1 egg
  • Hershey Kisses

    Instructions:

  1. Mix the cup of peanut butter, sugar, and one egg in a small bowl.
  2. In another small bowl fill with suger.
  3. Roll cookies into small balls and roll in suger until even coat of suger on all sides
  4. Spread parchment paper on baking sheet and place cookies
  5. Bake at 350Â° for 10-20 minutes
  6. Take out the cookies and let them cool for a few minuets then add 1 Hershey kiss in the center of the cookies.
  7. Eat and enjoy!
